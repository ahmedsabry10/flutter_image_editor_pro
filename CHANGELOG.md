## 1.0.0

* Initial release
* Crop & rotate with aspect ratio presets (free, 1:1, 4:3, 16:9, 9:16)
* 20 built-in color-matrix filters
* Custom filter support via ColorMatrix
* Adjust panel: brightness, contrast, saturation, warmth
* Freehand drawing with pen, brush, and eraser
* Text overlay with drag, font, color, size, shadow
* Sticker layers — any Flutter widget, drag/scale/rotate
* Unlimited undo/redo history
* Export as PNG or JPEG with custom quality
* Full-screen page via `ImageEditorPro.open()`
* Embedded widget via `ImageEditorWidget`
* Dark and light built-in themes + full custom theme support
* Pure Flutter — Android, iOS, Web, macOS, Windows, Linux
